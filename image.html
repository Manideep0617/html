instruction to cook white rice

Wash your rice.
Before you do anything,
rinse your rice off. ...
Measure your rice and water.
For most types of rice, you can use a water to rice ratio of 2:1. ...
Combine the rice and water in a pot.
Stir it and then put a lid on before turning the burner on high. Wait for it to boil. ... Let it cook.
